zenjournal App Go to App

A simple and distraction-free journalling app with a focus on privacy and ease of use.

Features

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • No sign up, no emails, just use it
  • Export / back up your data
  • Support for light/dark themes
  • No tracking or analytics - if you care to, please provide feedback via github issue.

Why another journalling app?

My 2021 new years resolution was to start journalling. All I wanted was a template I could fill in every day. Other journal apps either made this tedious or just couldn't do it.

zenjournal is my attempt to scratch a journalling itch.

Try it now

Roadmap

As I continue to journal, this app will change slightly. Some of the features I want to get around to adding are..

  • Search - search through all previous journal entries
  • Encryption - encrypt entries stored locally
  • Cloud Backup - background sync to cloud (encrypted locally)
